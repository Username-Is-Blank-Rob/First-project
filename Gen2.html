Chikorita is #152 in the national pokedex and number #001 in its regional pokedex. It is a Grass type pokemon and is known as the leaf Pokemon. Its average Height and weight are 2’11” and 14.1 lbs respectively. It evolves into Bayleef at level 16 and into Meganium at level 32. Totodile is #158 in the national pokedex and number #007 in its regional pokedex. It is a Water type pokemon and is known as the Big Jaw Pokemon. Its average Height and weight are 2’00” and 20.9 lbs respectively. It evolves into Croconaw at level 16 and into Feraligatr at level 32. Cyndaquil is #155 in the national pokedex and number #004 in its regional pokedex. It is a Fire type pokemon and is known as the Fire Mouse Pokemon. Its average Height and weight are 1’08” and 17.4 lbs respectively. It evolves into Quilava at level 16 and into Typhlosion at level 32.
