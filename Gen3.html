Treecko is #252 in the national pokedex and number #001 in its regional pokedex. It is a Grass type pokemon and is known as the Wood Gecko Pokemon. Its average Height and weight are 1’08” and 11.0 lbs respectively. It evolves into Grovyle at level 16 and into Sceptile at level 32. It is one of the few pokemon that has a mega evolution at the end of its evolutionary chain. Mudkip is #258 in the national pokedex and number #007 in its regional pokedex. It is a Water type pokemon and is known as the Mud Fish Pokemon. Its average Height and weight are 1’04” and 16.8 lbs respectively. It evolves into Marshtomp at level 16 and into Swampert at level 32. It is one of the few pokemon that has a mega evolution at the end of its evolutionary chain. Torchic is #255 in the national pokedex and number #004 in its regional pokedex. It is a Fire type pokemon and is known as the Chick Pokemon. Its average Height and weight are 1’04” and 5.5 lbs respectively. It evolves into Combusken at level 16 and into Blaziken at level 32. It is one of the few pokemon that has a mega evolution at the end of its evolutionary chain.
